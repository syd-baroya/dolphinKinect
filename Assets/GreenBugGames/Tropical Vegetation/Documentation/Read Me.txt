Tropical Vegetation (ver 1.1)

Versions: Unity 2018 or higher, included 2020.

The pack consists of 19 trees and 5 grasses, all objects have LOD and wind. (The wind on the HRDP works only with Unity 2019)
You can see the wind setting in the special scenes of each render. То standart render wind named as Prefab_Wind

HDRP version located folder and put on top of the main version, wind named as WindzoneHDRP. Diffusion profile you can fix on shader settings.
